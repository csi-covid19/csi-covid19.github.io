5-27 email

To George Sanchez, chair of the CSI chapter of the PSC,

We, the undersigned members of the CSI chapter of the PSC call for, under Article IX, section 7 of the PSC constitution, for a special meeting of our chapter. The purpose of the meeting is to debate and vote on the resolution attached below. It is crucial that we develop an effective response to the attacks that the CUNY administration and the Governor of New York are carrying out against our members, other CUNY workers, our students and the mission of our hallowed institution. An injury to one is an injury to all. This meeting should be scheduled for this week. Jay Arena, Chris Santiago, Ganesh Trichur, Jean Halley, Maryann Feola, Elizabeth Kane, Ozelm Goner, Alexander Zevin, Ismael Garcia-Colon, Ananya Mukherjea, Jeff Bussolini, Ilgin Erdem, Saadia Toor
