6/15 email

Dear Colleagues,

I write to update you on the activities of the UFS Research Foundation Faculty Advisory Council (RF-FAC).

I call your attention to minutes from meetings of the council, and several service incident reports brought to the attention of the leadership of the CUNY RF.

Of note is analysis of the 2018 Opinion Survey conducted by the Beta Corporation in the Spring of 2018. I also provide the spreadsheet, which includes the raw data, used in the analysis. Both the service incident reports and the analysis of the survey have been discussed with the leadership of the CUNY-RF; we await their responses.

Please continue to bring issues with the service provided by the CUNY RF to the attention of the Faculty Advisory Council.

Thank you. I hope that you are having a relaxing and productive summer. Best, -David

Wash your hands and maintain a "social distance."
